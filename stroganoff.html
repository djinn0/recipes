Stroganoff

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 8oz package mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups beef broth or 2 bouillon cubes disolved in water
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, diced
  • 10-20 frozen meatballs
  • 3 cups egg noodles
  • 2 tsp spicy mustard
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • Ground pepper to taste, approximatly 1-2 tsp
  • Thyme to taste, approximatly 1-2 tsp
  • Koshar salt to taste, approximatly 1-2 tsp

Cook down the mushrooms with the diced onions and garlic in 1/2 cup water. Make a roux
