Pupusas topped with coleslaw and salsa rosa

Pupusas

  • 1 cup Re-fried beans (canned or homemade)
  • 2 cups Maseca Instant Corn Masa Mix
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 cups Water
  • 1 cup Mozzarella cheese

Mix the 2 cups corn masa, 1 1/2 cups water and 1/4 tsp salt to form a dough. Make an egg sized ball of dough then flatten it and place approximatly 1 tbsp refried beans and 1/2 tbsp Mozzarella in the center. Fold the edges of the dough up around the beens and cheese then flatten again to form a Pupusa. Place the Pupusa in a hot frypan with the oil cook until browned on both sided then remove from the pan. Top the pupusas with the coleslaw and salsa rosa and serve.

Curtido, El Salvadorian Coleslaw

  • 2-3 cups Shredded Cabbage
  • 1 cup Shredded Carrots
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 Jalapeño, diced
  • 3 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar

  • a dash of dried Oregano
