Swedish Meatballs

Creamy sauce with warm and chewy meatballs make Swedish Meatballs a perfect comfort food. Swedish Meatballs are great dish because they can be quickly thrown together with frozen meatballs and common kitchen ingredients. They are a great as an appetizers or a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 10-20 Frozen Meatballs
  • 2 Tbs Butter
  • 2 Tbs Flour
  • 2 Cups Beef Broth
  • 3 Cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 2 Tsp Apple Cider Vineger or Lemon Juice
  • 1 Tbs Parsly
  • 1 Tsp Ground Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 Cup Cream

Brown the Meatballs remove from pan and set aside. Melt the butter in a the pan at medium heat and flour and mix until smooth to form a roux. Wisk in beef broth to form a sauce. Add garlic, parsly, salt, pepper, vinegar, and meatballs; simmer for 10 min to thicken. Remove from heat and stir in cream. Serve over mashed potatos or rice, with lingonberry jam.
